## FAQ SECTION

### Heading
**Frequently asked questions**

**Subheading:** Didn't find the answer you were looking for?
[Help center link]

#### How do I add an action button to my video?
Simply open the Interaction menu in our editor, select "Action Button," and drop it anywhere in your video. Customize the appearance, set your action (URL or scene jump), and you're done — no technical skills required.

#### Can I add multiple action buttons to one video?
Yes. Add as many action buttons as you need throughout your video. Use them for navigation, multiple CTAs, or building complex interactive experiences.

#### What can action buttons do?
Action buttons can open any URL in a new tab (perfect for CTAs and external links) or jump to different scenes within your video (great for navigation and non-linear content).

#### Can I customize the appearance of action buttons?
Absolutely. Customize colors, fonts, button size, rounded corners, shadows, stroke, opacity, and more. You can also apply your brand kit for consistent styling across all videos.

#### What are invisible buttons used for?
Invisible buttons are perfect for creating interactive screen captures and click-through demos. Place them over screenshots or recordings to build realistic product walkthroughs where users click through each step.

#### Can I track clicks on action buttons?
Yes. When you export with SCORM or use our analytics features, you can track viewer interactions including button clicks, helping you understand engagement and optimize your content.

#### Do action buttons work with translated videos?
Yes. Action buttons maintain their functionality across all translated versions of your videos, making it easy to create interactive experiences in multiple languages.

#### What's the pause-to-continue feature?
Coming soon: pause-to-continue (blocking) functionality will let you pause videos until viewers click specific action buttons — perfect for ensuring engagement with critical content in training and compliance videos.
